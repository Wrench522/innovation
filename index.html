Our Services

Cardiology

Our heart center offers comprehensive screenings, including ECGs, stress tests, and advanced imaging. We specialize in managing hypertension, heart failure, and preventative cardiovascular health to keep your heart beating strong.

Surgery

Equipped with state-of-the-art operating theaters, our surgical team performs everything from routine appendectomies to complex minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures, ensuring faster recovery times and patient safety.

Laboratory

Our fully automated diagnostic lab provides precise and rapid results. Services include hematology, biochemistry, and microbiology testing, helping our doctors make accurate clinical decisions for your treatment plan.
